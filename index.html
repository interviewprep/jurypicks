About Us

Our leadership team is of machine learning engineers, jury consultants and psychologists on a mission to democratize and revolutionize voir dire.

Jury consultants are currently affordable only in a tiny fraction of jury trials. And even jury consultants are engaged, voir dire is driven by subjective stereotypes, and with no measurable outcome.

We believe an effective voir dire is integral to trial by jury. By replacing subjective human intuition with Big data analytics and rigorous machine learning algorithms, we seek to make an effective, and quantifiable voir dire accessible to everyone.
